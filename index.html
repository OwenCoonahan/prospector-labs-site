The data layer for US energy infrastructure

47,000+ interconnection queue projects. 5.4 million DG installations. 6,593 developer profiles. 9 ISOs. Aggregated, enriched, and queryable — for investors, developers, and analysts.

The problem

US energy project data is scattered across 9 ISOs, dozens of state programs, and hundreds of government filings. Nobody has unified it. We aggregate 20+ public data sources into one system — then enrich it with tax credit eligibility, developer track records, and compliance screening so you can actually make decisions with it.

What you can do with it

The same data layer powers deal sourcing, due diligence, compliance, and programmatic access.

Source Investable Deals

Screen ITC-eligible projects with 50-70% effective credit rates. We cross-reference queue status, developer track records, energy community bonuses, and low-income overlays to surface opportunities that institutional capital misses. Pre-screened deal packages with credit analysis and developer profiles.

Project Due Diligence

Queue risk analysis, P10-P90 timeline distributions, developer benchmarks, POI congestion scoring, and comparable project analysis. Everything needed to evaluate an energy infrastructure deal — delivered in days, not weeks.

FEOC and Compliance Screening

IRA tax credits can stack to 50-70% — but only with the right eligibility data. We check energy community qualification (37% of projects eligible), low-income bonus (70% eligible), domestic content compliance, and FEOC exposure across equipment supply chains.

Energy Data API
